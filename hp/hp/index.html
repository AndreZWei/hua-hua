    How It Works

    To play HuaHua you need at least two players
    - although more than two players is gonna make it more fun!

    • Step 1

      Enter your nick name following this format:/nick my nickname

    • Step 2

      A painter will be assigned

    • Step 3

      The game will start when the painter types "ready".

    • Step 4

      The painter will then be given a word that is hidden form all other players to draw.

    • Step 5

      Players race to guess the right word.

    • Step 6

      Whenever a player guesses the word correctly, the next turn will start and a new painter will be assigned.

    • Step 7

      Every player should clear their own whiteboard and wait for the painter to type ready and start drawing.
